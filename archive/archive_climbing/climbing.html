Vanderbilt Climbing Team

I founded the Vanderbilt Climbing Club in 2017 and currently I'm the president and captain of the team competing in USA Climbing Collegiate Series. We have 130 club members and 20 team members (the team is a subset of the club). We're hoping to do well in the local competitions (TN, KY, NC region) and advance to Regionals Championships and then National Championships. I also climb outdoors a lot and it's always a great time (I'd recommend it for sure)!
