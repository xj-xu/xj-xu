Brief bio

Reader discretion: there may contain unsolicited information below.

I was born in the beautiful coastal city of Shenzhen and my family moved to another gorgeous coastal city Richmond when I was 8.
Growing up, I had lots of time to enjoy skateboarding, snowboarding, playing soccer and hockey. I was a huge Canucks fan... until they lost the cup in game 7 to the Bruins in 2011 (perhaps that shows I'm not true fan).

Then in 2011, I attended a sixth form college SCIE back in Shenzhen. (Not correlated to the Canucks' downfall)
At SCIE, I went through an UK curriculum of reading GCSE and A-Levels in physics, mathematics, biology, geography.
I continued to play soccer and captained the team. Near the end of sixth form, I picked up downhill skateboarding - a board sport I had not yet tried. It is quite a thrill and eventually I got good enough to be sponsored by a small local surf board company that also made longboards (Shenzhen Longboards but they stopped making longboards). I got to shoot some videos and go to races.
